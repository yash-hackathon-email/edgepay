How It Works

Three Steps to Send Money

No internet? No app ecosystem? No problem. EdgePay works where others can't.

01

Enter Details or Scan QR

Type the receiver's mobile number or UPI ID, or simply scan their QR code. The app auto-detects the payment mode.

02

Confirm & Authenticate

Review payment details, then enter your UPI PIN. The app builds the USSD command and dials it through your phone's native system.

03

Instant Confirmation

EdgePay monitors your SMS inbox for the bank's debit confirmation. Once detected, it shows "Payment Successful" instantly.
